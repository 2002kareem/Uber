## üìä Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Uber trip data, transforming raw transactional records into an interactive and insightful Power BI dashboard. The goal is to help business stakeholders understand booking trends, identify geographical hotspots, and optimize fleet management through data-driven insights.

## üöÄ Key Features
- **Dynamic KPIs:** Real-time tracking of Total Bookings, Revenue, Distance, and Average Ratings.
- **Location Intelligence:** Identification of the most frequent pickup and drop-off points to optimize driver allocation.
- **Temporal Analysis:** Breakdown of booking fluctuations by day, hour, and peak/off-peak periods.
- **User-Centric Design:** Advanced navigation using bookmarks and a seamless user experience.

## üìÇ Dashboard Structure
The report consists of three main analytical views:
1. **Overview Analysis:** General business health metrics and booking distribution by vehicle type.
2. **Location Analysis:** Deep dive into geographic hotspots and demand zones.
3. **Time Analysis:** Analysis of booking patterns over different time frames to identify peak hours.

## üí° Key Insights
- **Top Vehicle:** Uber X is the most utilized vehicle type, accounting for a significant portion of total bookings.
- **Peak Performance:** Identified specific days and hours with the highest demand, suggesting opportunities for surge pricing or driver incentives.
- **Hotspots:** Map visualizations pinpointed the exact locations with the highest turnaround rates.
